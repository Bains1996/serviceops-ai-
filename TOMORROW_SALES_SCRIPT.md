# Tomorrow Sales Script - Transport Companies

Use this exact script for calls, in-person meetings, or WhatsApp intro.

## 20-Second Intro
Hi, I am building ServiceOps AI for trucking carriers.
We help overnight dispatch teams reduce check-calls and assign next loads faster.
Drivers can text updates naturally, and managers keep approval control on high-impact actions.

## 60-Second Problem Framing
Most dispatch teams lose time in repetitive loops:
- where are you now
- unloaded yet
- what next
- ETA changed
- who told customer

That causes missed windows, dispatcher overload, and weak shift handoffs.

## 60-Second Solution
Our agent takes routine communication and load-state updates off dispatcher shoulders.
When a driver is free, it suggests next best loads based on timing, fit, and economics.
If there is cost or service risk, it routes to manager approval first.
Everything is logged for clear handoff.

## Offer
We run a focused paid pilot for one terminal and one shift.
In 6-8 weeks, we target:
- fewer check-calls
- faster reassignment
- fewer missed windows
- cleaner overnight handoff

## Discovery Close
Would you be open to a 30-minute call this week to map your overnight flow and see if this fits your operation?

## Objection Handling
### We already have a TMS
Perfect. We do not replace your TMS.
We automate the communication and decision layer around it.

### Drivers do not use apps
No problem. SMS-first workflow.
Drivers text naturally, no app training required initially.

### We cannot risk errors
High-impact actions stay approval-gated.
Routine updates are automated, decisions remain accountable.

### Too expensive
Pilot pricing is tied to measurable dispatch workload and service improvement.
If the KPI improvement is not there, we should not scale.

## Final Ask
Can we schedule a 30-minute workflow call on:
- Option A: tomorrow
- Option B: next business day
